Play Child Safety Standards Policy


"Play Skyblue app is committed to providing a safe and positive environment for all users, especially children. We strictly prohibit any content that could be considered harmful to minors, including graphic violence, sexual exploitation, or discriminatory language. Developers must ensure that all are designed with age-appropriate features and mechanics, and robust parental controls are available to manage time and access to specific content.

1. Compliance with Laws:

Ensure the policy aligns with relevant child protection laws and regulations in your region.


2. Enforcement Mechanism:

Establish clear processes for reporting violations and taking appropriate actions against offenders


3. Prohibited Content:

No depiction of graphic violence, including excessive gore or realistic portrayals of child abuse.


No sexually suggestive content involving minors, including explicit language or imagery


No content promoting harmful stereotypes based on race, ethnicity, gender, or sexual orientation.


No content that encourages dangerous activities like drug use, self-harm, or reckless behavior.
